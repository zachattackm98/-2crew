Additional Charges

  • Initial Cleanup Fee: For yards that have not been cleaned in over a month, add a one-time fee.
    • Less than 1 month: No additional fee.
    • 1 to 3 months: + $20
    • More than 3 months: + $40
  • Extra Large Yards: For yards larger than 1 acre, add an additional $10 - $20 per visit, or provide a custom quote.
  • Travel Surcharge: If a customer is located outside the primary service area, you might consider adding a travel surcharge.
  • Add-on Services:
    • Deodorizing/Sanitizing: Charge per visit.
    • Waste Removal: Charge per visit.
    • Lawn Mowing: Provide combined service pricing.
    • Flea and Tick Control: Price per application.
    • Pet Waste Composting: Price per month.
    • Gutter Cleaning: Price per visit.
    • Garbage Can Cleaning: Price per can.

Notes:

  • Free Initial Cleanup: Factor this into your pricing (e.g., by slightly increasing the price of the regular service).
  • Pricing Page: Outline these costs on your website's "Pricing" page.
  • Quote Form: Your online quote form should calculate the total cost based on selections.
  • Flexibility: Be prepared to provide custom quotes for unusual situations.
